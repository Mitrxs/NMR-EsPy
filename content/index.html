Welcome to NMR-EsPy’s Documentatation¶

Simon G. Hulse & Mohammadali Foroozandeh,
 Department of Chemistry,
 University of Oxford

NMR-EsPy (Nuclear Magnetic Resonance Estimation in Python) is a Python package for the estimation of parameters that describe NMR signals.

As with the majority of packages that do a lot of number crunching in Python, NMR-EsPy relies heavily on NumPy as well as SciPy routines to function efficiently.

The NMR-EsPy package is a product of work carried out during Simon’s Ph.D within the Foroozandeh group. To find out more about the group, take a look at our website.

If you come across any unexpected behavior, please get in touch with Simon, via email (see the email icon in the sidebar), or file an issue.
